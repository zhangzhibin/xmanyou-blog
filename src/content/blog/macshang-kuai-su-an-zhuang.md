<!--kg-card-begin: markdown--><!--kg-card-begin: markdown--><p>Sequel Pro号称是Mac上最好用的MySql管理工具，而且是免费且开源的。<br>
然而，从官网 （<a href="http://www.sequelpro.com/">http://www.sequelpro.com/</a> ）下载仅10M大小的Sequel Pro，居然异常的慢。</p>
<p>怎么办呢？<br>
可以尝试一下通过brew来安装。命令如下：</p>
<blockquote>
<p>brew cask install sequel-pro</p>
</blockquote>
<p>反正我很快就下载完了，而通过官网才刚下了10%。</p>
<p><img src="/content/images/2018/03/1521642193606.jpg" alt="1521642193606"></p>
<p>对了，如果你还没有装caskroom，可以先安装:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>brew tap caskroom/cask</p>
</blockquote>
<p>这是一个homebrew的扩展，官方宣传语是(<a href="https://caskroom.github.io/">https://caskroom.github.io/</a>)：</p>
<blockquote>
<p>Homebrew-Cask extends Homebrew and brings its elegance, simplicity, and speed to macOS applications and large binaries alike.</p>
<p>It only takes 1 line in your shell to reach 3941 Casks maintained by 5359 contributors.</p>
</blockquote>
